About me
The world is digital.
The success of your business is increasingly dependent on your "digital footprint" - i.e. all of the things that tell your prospects and customers who you are, what you do and why they should want to to do business with you.

Our clients are intentional about their footprint and we help them achieve that intention.

You have a footprint.
Is it intentional or an accident?

The people who you do business with use Google to find out who you are and how you do business. Often, they know about your professional experience, who your co-workers were and how you treated your customers. It's impossible to control every aspect of the digital world but a measured and thorough approach makes sense.

Your message and value proposition are important components of your ability to compete. Take control of it and make it work to your advantage.
Business Insider Magazine says this in a 2012 report:

"There are approximately 310 million people in the United States. Of these people, approximately 250 million are over the age of 14. According to Comscore, about 235 million of these Americans use mobile devices It seems likely that almost all of these folks will eventually have smartphones. This creates a "total addressable market" for smartphones of about 225 million people in the U.S."

Not to mention the desktop and laptop computers that they use. Wouldn't you like to talk with them in a focused, productive way?
